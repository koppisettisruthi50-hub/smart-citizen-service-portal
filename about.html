About the Project

Problem Statement

Many citizens do not know which documents are required to apply for government certificates and welfare schemes. Different offices may provide inconsistent information, leading to confusion and multiple visits.

Our Solution

The Smart Citizen Service Portal provides a centralized platform where users can easily find the required documents, eligibility criteria, fees, and other important information related to certificates and government schemes.
