Government Schemes

Old Age Pension

Eligibility: Senior citizens meeting income criteria.

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Age Proof
  • Bank Passbook
  • Passport Size Photos

Fee: Free

Widow Pension

Eligibility: Widowed women meeting eligibility criteria.

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Husband Death Certificate
  • Bank Passbook
  • Passport Size Photos

Fee: Free

YSR Cheyutha

Eligibility: Eligible women beneficiaries as per scheme guidelines.

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Passbook
  • Ration Card

Fee: Free

YSR Rythu Bharosa

Eligibility: Eligible farmers of Andhra Pradesh.

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land Documents
  • Bank Passbook

Fee: Free
